About us







Welcome to FitMe gym located in the heart of Stockholm, Sweden.Vision We lead people to a healthier life no matter who you are or where you live.

We at FitMe are keen that exercise and well-being should be available to everyone. By running an online and offline Fitness business, with the ambition to constantly develop and expand, we thus open the doors during all 4am to 12am and invite you to exercise.

Mission Our mission is to provide the best value option for our customers, thus we provide onling training, group training and personal training for anyone that wants gym instructor. We will do this through availability, hospitality, and functionality at an attractive price. We are proud to be available to our members by offering training opportunities. Hospitality is about acting friendly and welcoming to our members and we do this by putting our members in focus. For us, it is important that our members feel that they are seen, heard and appreciated. It is together with our members that we create the best training environment and we always strive for our members to take away positive impressions from the gym. As a continuation of this hospitality, including through the friendly treatment, we always strive for our gyms to be functional. By this we mean that machines, weights, changing rooms etc. should work without any problems. It should be simple, smooth and fun to train with us and this is something we work with daily to maintain.

An attractive price is something we are proud to offer and it has always been important to us to show that training should be something for everyone and hence our choice of price. Our members should feel that we offer a lot, including group training and that we are open at long hour. By offering what we mentioned above, we have competitive gyms. This is reinforced by our skilled employees who together build relationships with our members. It is the employees who work at our gyms who contribute to a healthy lifestyle and, above all, they contribute to a fun and social training environment.

our values At FitMe Gym, we want to contribute to better health and that it should happen on our members' terms. By this we mean that everyone should have the opportunity to train - at an attractive price. The pace is fast, but above all it is a social, developing and driven environment. We always strive to become even better in order to offer our members what we believe is the best training concept, and together we set ambitious goals that we work together to achieve. Our values define how we work at the company and can be compared to Fitme gym's core. When we interact, colleagues, or with our members, our values help us grow. We believe that the joint approach within the company contributes to our attracting new colleagues and new members around the world.

Long term thinking We believe that decisions should be based on long-term thinking, even if it is at the expense of short-term financial gain. FitMe is here to stay and we make decisions that go along with it. Our company is built on the foundation of helping people live a healthier lifestyle, and a healthy lifestyle is never something that happens overnight. On the member's terms Everything starts with our members and therefore we strive to always evaluate our performance and how we can work to make our members happy. It is together with our members that the best training environment is created and it is therefore important to us that our members are in focus.

Learning organization We want to be a learning organization that actively evaluates our operations to create opportunities for improvement. It is through our lessons that we can improve and develop in the future. We see lessons learned as a key factor to being able to improve and to remain a competitive Fitness business in the market.

Collaboration creates the team We support each other in the work we do and we believe in what we want to achieve. Collaborating between different departments and exchanging knowledge makes us more efficient, it enables us to have a deeper understanding of each other and also the opportunity to learn and develop.

Social responsibility We at FitMe want to contribute to creating a healthier and better life, and we want to be involved and make a difference for those who live in tough conditions. We are involved and contribute to make a difference.
