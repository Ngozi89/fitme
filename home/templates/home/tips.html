Working out is fun, but working out together is even more fun. Feel the music and unleash your energy together with our enthusiastic instructors. At FitMe we offer group training sessions. Whether you want to get stronger, faster or perhaps more mobile, there is a session for you. We are constantly working on developing our group training and there are always new exciting group training sessions.

Why should I try group training?

There are many advantages to group training and it is above all the community with other members that makes many people appreciate this form of training. Exercising together in a group is not only fun, it also increases performance according to several studies. For those who find it difficult to find the inspiration for training, group training can be a good start. Here you don't have to think about what to do, just follow the instructor's instructions. If you are a person who often cancels training at the last minute, group training can also be a smart thing to try.
